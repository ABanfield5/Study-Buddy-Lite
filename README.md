# Study-Buddy-Lite
Study Buddy Lite is an AI tutor that helps you learn anything faster and more easily.

AI-Powered Learning Assistant (GPT Version)

## What It Does:
• Explains topics in a simple way.
• Helps you study and practice.
• Answers questions step-by-step.
• Breaks down confusing ideas.

## Who It's For:
• Students.
• People learning a new subject.
• Anyone who needs better understanding with clear explanations.

## How It Works:
Study Buddy Lite is built as a custom AI (GPT) that is designed to teach, guide, and support you while learning.

## Goal:
• Make learning easier, clearer, and more accessible for anyone.
• Help people understand difficult concepts through simple explanations.
